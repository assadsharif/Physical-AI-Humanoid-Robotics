## Key Concepts

- **Physical Intelligence**: The capacity to interact effectively with the physical world
- **Morphological Intelligence**: Intelligence derived from body structure and dynamics
- **Environmental Coupling**: The bidirectional influence between agent and environment

---

:::warning Safety Note
Physical AI systems must account for the unpredictability of real-world environments. Design principles must always prioritize safe failure modes.
:::
